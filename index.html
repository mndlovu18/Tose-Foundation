About

Tose Foundation is a non-profit organization that was founded in September, 2020 by four college students from various backgrounds and majors who are passionate about providing access to educational opportunities to students from less privileged backgrounds in Zimbabwe.

The name of the organization is in Shona which means “together” because we believe human intelligence is not a race with one track and one golden cup. It differs as a natural meadow and works like an ecosystem where we all thrive because of understanding and embracing our differences. We can use our diversity of ideas, talents, and resources to support each other to reach our full potential. As Tose we are committed to:

  • Host a Fellows Program that will run for 9 weeks for high achieving, low income students in Zimbabwe.
  • Provide to our fellows an all rounded access to life skills and development opportunities which include having life long mentors and access to an efficient alumni network.
  • Shorten the bridge between unemployment and corruption in Zimbabwe by providing an opportunity for equal access to open attachment/internship opportunities.

Peer Mentorship

All our fellows will have a life long access to mentorship through our alumni network and partner organizations

Entrepreneurship

We seek to inspire an entrepreneurial mindset on our fellows so that they imerge to be proactive leaders who will effect real change in Zimbabwe

Coding Lessons

Fellows will be equipped with relevant skills to empower them to bring technological innovations in their communities

All About Finance Classes

We aim to bring finance literacy among our fellows and allow them to explore careers in finace

Resume and LinkedIn Workshops

Fellows will recieve coaching on how to build the perfect resume and learn how to effectively network on Linkedin

Sex Education

As part of the workshops that our fellows will attend will be a sex education workshop that is meant to make them more aware of the subject

Mental Health

Experts in mental health will be a part of each cohort to teach our fellows about mental health and it's importance in their education

Internship Advertisements

Tose will serve as a platform for businesses and organizations in Zimbabwe to advertise their internship opportunities
