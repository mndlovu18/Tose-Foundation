About Us

Tose Foundation is a non-profit organization on a mission to create a level playing field between students in underfunded schools and private, well-established schools In Zimbabwe. We are committed to introducing our fellows to a holistic experience that includes:

  • Giving our fellows acces to learning opportunites in Finance and Technology.
  • A career accelerator program that will provide Tose fellows with necessary skills to stand out in job search through resume and personal branding workshops
  • Give our fellows access to life-long mentors who will help them navigate their career path
