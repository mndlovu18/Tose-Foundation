TOSE TEAM

Jordan Mubako

Head of Marketing and Communications

Jordan is a 22 year old rising senior at Harvard University where he studies Economics on the Pre-Med track. He was born in Harare but lived in Bulawayo for most of his high school years. His life's goal is to contribute positively to the development of Zimbabwe. After graduation, he wants to go to Medical school and then work as a doctor back home. This summer of 2021, he is carrying out Grant-Funded research on the upregulation of proteins in the gastrointestinal tract at Boston Children’s Hospital. Jordan is also interested in finance, and he is currently working for a Boston-based hedge fund to learn about investing. Jordan is passionate about helping the youth of Zimbabwe gain access to educational opportunities.

Sibongumusa Ncube

Head of Writing

Sibongumusa Ncube is a sophomore International Relations student at Africa University, in Zimbabwe. As a Yale Young African Scholars alum, Sibongumusa has previously partnered with Higher Life Education and Education Matters to mentor high school students from disenfranchised communities. Besides advocacy for universal access to educational opportunities, Sibongumusa is also passionate about gender equality activism and regularly participates in Women’s Democratic Network programs for young women interested in politics and policy creation as a career path. Being a student at a PanAfricanist institution has encouraged Sibongumusa to be a part of initiatives such as Tose where young African students lead the charge in reshaping the accessibility of education.

Tapiwa Dzviriri

Head of Finance

Tapiwa is an undergraduate student at the National University of Science and Technology in Zimbabwe where he is majoring in Actuarial Science.Tapiwa is passionate about business, youth empowerment, and education. He loves combining his passions with his eclectic problem solving skills to find innovative solutions to some of the most challenging issues and problems around him. Tapiwa has played instrumental roles and made significant impact in organizations and campaigns that he has been a part of such Pora Health and The Precious You. He is a strong believer of talent identification and development amongst the youth even those in marginalized areas as the main tool for success. Thus, he treasures the opportunity to achieve this by joining the board of Tose Foundation.
