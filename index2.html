EQUALIZING ACCESS
TO OPPORTUNITIES

Tose Foundation is a non-profit organization on a mission to create a level playing field between students in underfunded schools in Zimbabwe and those in private, well-established schools.As Tose, we are committed to introducing our fellows to:

  • An all-rounded education that includes education in the finance and technology industry
  • Career accelerator program that coaches our fellows to apply to life-changing opportunities available in Zimbabwe and the international community
  • Give our fellows access to life-long mentors who will help them navigate their career path
