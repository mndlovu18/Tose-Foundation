OUR PROGRAM

Ulwazi Fellowship Program

Background

The state of education in Zimbabwe is badly in need of innovation. As the years go on the educational gap widens leaving more and more Zimbabwean youth out in the cold. The immediate problem is access to resources. Although Zimbabwe boasts one of the highest textbook availability ratios, the current status quo has put a tragic ceiling on achievement for many young Zimbabweans. They go diligently to underfunded schools but are not allowed access to key resources that are needed in the 21st Century.

Program Plan

We want to provide resources to students that need them most. We believe providing these youth with programs tailor-made for their success. We plan to bolster the education these students receive not just by giving them resources to support what they are already learning, but by providing a whole new set of skills that will serve them in the long run. These skills include dynamic writing, computer science and entrepreneurial leadership.

We also plan to provide students with accessible pathways for career development and mentorship. Iron sharpens iron and here at TOSE, we are dedicated to providing these avid learners with everything and everyone they need to succeed. We draw from a pool of talented achievers at tertiary institutions both abroad and at home. With TOSE, promising young students will find big Sisters and Brothers who will guide them on their path to reaching their goals.
