GET INVOLVED

"Together we stand and together we Rise"

Tose is a nonprofit organization, which means the extent of the work we do relies heavily on the generosity of our donors and our partners. We appreciate your generous donations and support of our work. Should you choose to donate, know that all the money we get goes towards bridging the accessibility gap and the digital divide that holds our participants back. All funds are channelled towards providing internet access and various other educational tools to our exceptionally gifted cohorts. By clicking on the link below, you lend a hand in shaping the future of these students, the students we are counting on to shape the future of Africa. No amount is too small; every little bit counts.
