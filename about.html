WHO WE ARE

More about US

Our Name: Tose is a Shona word meaning “together” because we believe human intelligence is not a race with a definite end; it is a winding road on an infinite journey. On this journey companions are not only helpful, but necessary. Together, we use the diversity of our ideas, talents, and resources to support each other to reach our full potential. Nothing great can be accomplished by individuals. It takes a village to raise a child and it takes a group of concerned Zimbabweans to institute tangible change in our country. Together we stand and together we Rise!

Mission Statement: To provide unlimited paths to success for students from underfunded schools and orphanages through career development services and access to educational programs.

Vision Statement: To build a generation of holistic skilled leaders who are well equipped to impact positive change in their communities.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Teamwork - At Tose, we value collaboration. We work together, bringing in our different experiences and skills to ensure that we are all successful and no one is left behind.

  • Service - Serving others is at the core of our foundation and that comes in different forms. Service through knowledge is a great way to empower our nation and communities.

  • Integrity - We are an open organization and encourage openness and vulnerability in all facets of our work. Open communication between our stakeholders to create an environment of trust, encourage sharing of ideas and achieve great results.
